This text is about a new type of numbers in programming,

numbers and they are great in terms of accuracy,
performance, range, and precision.
This makes them very suitable for computer graphics programming,
sound processing, and scientific programming.

Arabic:

"Fixed-point" في البرمجة هو نوع من
 الأرقام الذي يوزع
 النقطة العشرية على 
مكان محدد من الأرقام
، على عكس الأرقام 
العشرية التي لا يوزع
 عليها نقطة العشرية في مكان محدد.

English:

"Fixed-point" in programming is 
a type of number that has the 
decimal point placed at a fixed 
location within the number, 
unlike floating-point numbers
 which don't have a fixed decimal place.
